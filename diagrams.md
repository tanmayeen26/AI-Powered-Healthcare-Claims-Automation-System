## Project Snapshot

**Project Type:** Enterprise Transformation / Healthcare Operations  
**Domain:** Healthcare Claims Processing  
**Focus Areas:** AI, Program Delivery, Business Requirements, Process Automation, Analytics

### Business Challenge
Healthcare claims teams often face:
- Long claim turnaround times
- High manual effort
- Claim denials and rework
- Limited end-to-end visibility
- Complex compliance and audit requirements

### Proposed Solution
The solution combines:
- **AI Business Requirements Builder** to auto-generate BRDs, epics, user stories, and acceptance criteria
- **Claims Validation & Adjudication Engine** to automate claim decisions
- **Fraud Detection Module** to identify anomalies and suspicious patterns
- **Operational Dashboard** to monitor KPIs, claim status, and risk alerts

## Features

### 1) AI-Powered Requirements Builder
- Converts stakeholder input into BRDs, epics, user stories, and acceptance criteria
- Suggests gaps, dependencies, and test considerations
- Speeds up requirements discovery and improves consistency

### 2) Intelligent Claims Automation
- Validates incoming claims for completeness and eligibility
- Uses rules + AI to classify and adjudicate claims
- Routes exceptions to the right operational teams

### 3) Fraud Detection
- Flags unusual patterns and suspicious claims
- Supports compliance review and audit readiness

### 4) Real-Time Reporting
- Tracks denial rate, turnaround time, SLA adherence, and automation rate
- Provides operational visibility for program and business stakeholders

## Sample User Stories

- As a **claims processor**, I want AI to auto-classify claims so that I can reduce manual effort.
- As a **compliance analyst**, I want fraud alerts so that I can proactively review high-risk claims.
- As a **stakeholder**, I want real-time dashboards so that I can monitor claim health and operational KPIs.
- As a **business analyst**, I want AI-generated requirements drafts so that I can accelerate documentation quality and speed.

## Risks and Mitigations

| Risk | Mitigation |
|------|------------|
| Poor source data quality | Add validation and exception rules at intake |
| AI output inconsistency | Use templates, review workflows, and human approval |
| Resistance to change | Build training, phased rollout, and adoption support |
| Compliance concerns | Maintain audit trail, approvals, and rule transparency |
